1. Introduction

Laam Token is a digital asset built on the Stellar Blockchain, designed to simplify payments, remittances, and financial services for the global community.

Laam aims to function as a native token within the Stellar ecosystem, combining speed, security, and low transaction costs to enable an inclusive digital economy.

2. Problem & Solution

Current Problems

High costs and delays in cross‑border transfers.
Limited access to modern financial services.
Instability and low trust in some local currencies.

Laam Solution

Near‑instant settlement globally on Stellar.
Extremely low network fees suitable for micro‑payments.
Security and transparency on a proven public blockchain.
