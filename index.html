1. Introduction

Laam Token is a digital asset built on the Stellar Blockchain, designed to simplify payments, remittances, and financial services for the global community. Laam aims to become a native token within the Stellar ecosystem, combining speed, security, and low transaction costs to enable a more inclusive digital economy.

2. Problem & Solution

Problems:

  • High cost of sending money.
  • Limited access to financial services.
  • Lack of trust in unstable currencies.

Solution:

  • Near-instant global transactions.
  • Extremely low transaction fees.
  • Built on Stellar – a trusted, global blockchain.

4. Use Cases

  • Remittances: Fast and low-cost money transfers across borders.
  • P2P Crypto & Fiat Trading: Enabling peer-to-peer exchange between crypto and local fiat currencies.
  • Marketplace Payments: Usable within Laam Market for housing, cars, and rentals.
  • E-commerce & Gaming: A currency for digital apps, services, and online economies.

5. Advantages of Laam Token

  • Speed: Transactions settle within seconds.
  • Low Fees: Significantly cheaper than traditional banking.
  • Security: Powered by Stellar, a reliable blockchain.
  • Accessibility: Primarily available on the Laam Wallet, and also supported by Stellar-compatible wallets (Lobstr, Solar, Albedo, etc.).

6. Roadmap

  • Laam Token launch, Listing on Stellar DEX
  • Launch of Laam Market (real estate & vehicles), Laam P2P (Crypto ↔ Fiat trading)
  • Expanded wallet & exchange integrations, Mobile money & banking partnerships
  • Laam ecosystem expansion (apps, e-commerce, gaming), Global partnerships

7. Token Distribution

  • Community & Users: 50%
  • Liquidity & Exchanges: 25%
  • Ecosystem Development: 15%
  • Founders & Team: 10%

8. Conclusion

Laam Token represents the future of digital finance for the global community. Built on Stellar, Laam offers fast, low-cost, and secure transactions with real-world usability.
